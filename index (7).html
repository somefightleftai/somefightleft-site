Who's Behind This

I'm Michael. I turn 62 in July. Weekdays I wake up at 4am, do yoga, hit a strength & conditioning class before work, Jiu Jitsu at lunch, and Muay Thai in the evening. Weekends are boxing, rock climbing, BMX, more S&C — and time with my boys. I've been doing yoga and martial arts for over 35 years. People everywhere I train ask me how I look this way. Some Fight Left is the unfiltered answer — and proof that the best years don't have to be behind you.
